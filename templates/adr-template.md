## Context

1–3 sentences: what situation forced this decision.

## Decision

1–3 sentences: what we chose.

## Business Reason

1 sentence: why this matters beyond the code.

## Consequences

1–3 sentences: what becomes easier, what becomes harder, what we now depend on. Every decision has consequences (Nygard); "none worth noting" must be written explicitly, not implied by omission.
